Company Profile

Engineering Digital Scale for the Enterprise Era

For over 14 years, SoftKiwi has been the strategic engineering partner for global brands, delivering secure, cloud-native architectures and mission-critical software.

Our Philosophy

Architecting Resilience. Delivering Scale.

SoftKiwi was founded on a core engineering principle: enterprise software must be highly available, deeply secure, and built for longevity. We don't just deliver code; we architect technical foundations that empower businesses to operate without friction.

We view technology not as a commodity, but as a strategic asset. By aligning advanced engineering with your core business logic, we eliminate technical debt and build platforms that adapt to your future.

A Decade of Engineering Excellence Since 2012

Founded in 2012, SoftKiwi began with a singular focus on robust web engineering. Over the past 14+ years, we have systematically evolved into a global technology powerhouse and an Authorized AWS Partner. Every infrastructure we deploy—be it a complex e-commerce ecosystem, a custom ERP integration, or a secure enterprise web application—is executed with rigorous technical precision.

Headquartered in India's premier tech hub (Spaze i-Tech Park, Gurgaon), our delivery centers provide world-class engineering to a global clientele. From modernizing monolithic legacy systems to deploying advanced Applied AI and scalable cloud infrastructures, our trajectory is defined by a relentless commitment to code quality, data security, and client success.
