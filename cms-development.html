Today’s enterprise digital strategies require delivering unified content and commerce experiences across multiple touchpoints—web, mobile, IoT, and digital displays. Traditional monolithic CMS platforms often bottleneck this scale, creating security vulnerabilities and slow load times. At SoftKiwi, we engineer decoupled, headless CMS and e-commerce architectures that separate backend logic from the frontend presentation.

By combining powerful content APIs (Contentful, Sanity, Headless WordPress) and commerce engines (Shopify Plus, Adobe Commerce) with highly optimized frontends (Next.js, React), we provide your marketing team with total editorial control while empowering your engineering team with a secure, sub-second latency infrastructure. The result? Infinite scalability and unmatched omnichannel delivery.

Our Architecture Lifecycle

Phase 01

Content Modeling & Taxonomy

We audit your operational workflows and blueprint a modular, highly structured content architecture designed for reuse across multiple digital platforms.

Phase 02

Headless API Engineering
